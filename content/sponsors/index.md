### My Work

For **20 years** I worked to keep the Ruby Community vibrant.

That meant speaking at [**conferences**](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=luca+guidi+ruby), creating [**screencasts**](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCAprzjCQpYdzyN9Bv-Iq1sA), writing [**blog posts**](/), and playing a role in [**Open Source**](https://github.com/jodosha).

Over those years I contributed to important projects like [Ruby on Rails](https://github.com/rails/rails), [Rack](https://github.com/rack/rack), [redis-rb](https://github.com/redis/redis-rb), and [lego](https://github.com/go-acme/lego), and created my own: [Hanami](https://github.com/hanami) and [redis-store](https://github.com/redis-store). I was part of the [dry-rb](https://github.com/dry-rb) core team, and led [Hanami 2](https://github.com/hanami/hanami/issues/1067) and [Hanami::API 1](https://github.com/hanami/api). 🌸

I've since moved on from that community, but everything above is still out there, free to use.
